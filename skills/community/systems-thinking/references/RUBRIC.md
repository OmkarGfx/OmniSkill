# Rubric — Systems Thinking (0–2 per dimension)

Score each dimension 0/1/2. Total max = 20. Suggested passing bar: **>= 16/20**.

## 1) Triggering + scope (0–2)
- **0:** No trigger phrases; scope and non-scope are missing; unclear when to use the skill.
- **1:** Some trigger phrases exist; "when to use / not use" is present but neighbor skills are not referenced.
- **2:** Realistic trigger phrases; clear "when to use / not use" with explicit redirects to neighboring skills.

## 2) Boundary + success measures (0–2)
- **0:** No system boundary defined; no metrics or guardrails; analysis scope is unbounded.
- **1:** Boundary is stated but too broad or too narrow; metrics exist but are not measurable or time-bound.
- **2:** Boundary is tight enough to act on and wide enough to capture key externalities; metrics are explicit, measurable, and time-bound.

## 3) Actors + incentives realism (0–2)
- **0:** No actors listed, or actors are generic labels without incentives/constraints/power.
- **1:** Key actors are listed with some incentives, but power dynamics or "invisible" actors (policies, culture, platform) are missing.
- **2:** Actors have explicit incentives, constraints, and power; includes 1-2 invisible actors; conflicts and likely behaviors are captured.

## 4) System map quality (0–2)
- **0:** No system map; or map is a list of buzzwords without causal links.
- **1:** Variables and links exist but some links lack direction, mechanism, or testability; map has fewer than 8 or more than 25 links without prioritization.
- **2:** 10-20 high-signal links; each link is directional, states the mechanism, and is testable; time delays are marked.

## 5) Feedback loops + delays (0–2)
- **0:** No feedback loops identified; dynamics are treated as linear cause-effect chains.
- **1:** Loops are named but lack a "so what" narrative or miss key delays that create overshoot/oscillation.
- **2:** 2-6 reinforcing/balancing loops identified; each has a "so what" narrative; key delays are captured with estimated lag times.

## 6) Second-/third-order effects (0–2)
- **0:** Only first-order effects listed; no winners/losers analysis; no mitigations.
- **1:** Some second-order effects noted but ledger is incomplete; mitigations or guardrails are vague.
- **2:** Effects ledger covers first through third order for top 1-3 decisions; includes winners/losers, at least one unintended consequence per move, and specific mitigations.

## 7) Leverage points + interventions (0–2)
- **0:** No leverage points identified; interventions are generic ("improve communication").
- **1:** Leverage points named but interventions lack owners, sequencing, or measurable indicators.
- **2:** Interventions are tied to specific loops/leverage points, have owners, measurable leading indicators, guardrails, and are sequenced.

## 8) System-build opportunity (0–2)
- **0:** No system-build / automation opportunity identified for recurring pain.
- **1:** Opportunity mentioned but lacks a concrete first step, owner, or measurable outcome.
- **2:** Converts recurring pain into a concrete process/tool/automation with an owner, first step, and expected outcome.

## 9) Decision usability (0–2)
- **0:** Analysis is academic; does not produce a decision-ready narrative; tradeoffs and uncertainties are buried.
- **1:** Narrative exists but tradeoffs or uncertainties are implicit; a reader needs a live meeting to understand implications.
- **2:** Decision-ready narrative with explicit tradeoffs, uncertainties, and recommended next steps; a reader can act without a live meeting.

## 10) Safety + robustness (0–2)
- **0:** No rollback/stop conditions; overconfident assertions where data is missing; secrets or sensitive data exposed.
- **1:** Some safety considerations but rollback triggers are vague or uncertainty is understated.
- **2:** Rollback/stop conditions are specific; uncertainty is clearly flagged; no secrets; assumptions are labeled with confidence levels.
